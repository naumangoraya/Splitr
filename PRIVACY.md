# Privacy Policy — Splitr

_Last updated: 29 June 2026_

Splitr ("the app") is an expense-splitting app published by **Eidosyne**. This
policy explains what data the app collects, why, and your choices. Questions:
**nauman.noor@triplek.tech**.

> Owner: confirm the contact email and the "published by" name before submitting
> to the Play Store, and update the date whenever this policy changes.

## What we collect
- **Account details** — your email address and display name, used to sign you in
  and show who you are to people you share groups with.
- **Expense & balance data** — the groups you create or join, expenses, who paid,
  how costs are split, settlements, and the resulting balances.
- **Messages** — chat messages you send within a group or to a friend, including
  any expense you choose to reference in a message.
- **Push token** — if you enable notifications, a device push token so we can
  alert you about new expenses, settlements, and messages.

We do **not** collect your contacts, location, photos, or device identifiers
beyond the push token, and we do not use advertising or third-party analytics.

## How we use it
Only to operate the app: authenticate you, sync your groups and balances across
your devices and the people you share with, deliver chat and notifications, and
keep your data available when you sign in again.

## Where it is stored
Your data is stored in our backend hosted on **Supabase** (Postgres), protected
by per-user access rules so you only see groups you belong to. Push delivery uses
**Google Firebase Cloud Messaging**. We do not sell or share your data with third
parties for their own purposes.

## Data retention & deletion
Your data is kept while your account is active. To delete your account and its
associated data, email **nauman.noor@triplek.tech** and we will remove it.

## Children
Splitr is not directed to children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect their
data.

## Changes
We may update this policy; material changes will be reflected by the date above.
